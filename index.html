The Gap

Kenya's most promising enterprises sit in a financing no-man's land. Too large and complex for microfinance. Too early or too asset-light for commercial banks. Too operationally hands-on for passive private equity. The result is a structural credit gap that costs businesses growth, jobs, and time. Ratelrock was built specifically to close it.

Our Positioning

We operate at the intersection of private credit and business advisory because we believe capital without capability is incomplete. A business that receives a facility it is not yet structured to absorb does not grow, it struggles. Our two practice lines are deliberately integrated: the credit informs the advisory, and the advisory makes the credit work.

Our Commitment

We are not a generalist lender with a Kenya desk. We are specialists, built from the ground up for this market, this stage of business, and this moment in East Africa's private capital development. Every decision we make reflects that specificity.
