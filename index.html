Who We Serve

Your business is complex. Your capital should match that complexity. Ratelrock provides structured debt facilities and active business transformation support to enterprises that require a specialist credit partner — one that takes the time to understand your business model, your cash flow cycle, and your growth ambitions. We don't just provide capital. We work alongside you.

Ratelrock offers institutional investors access to a large, structurally underserved private credit market in Kenya — with target net IRRs of 18–26% (USD-hedged), a net loss rate of 3–5% after recoveries, and a disciplined underwriting framework built to perform across macro cycles.
