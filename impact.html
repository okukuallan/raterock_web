Four Pillars

Our commitment
to responsible capital.

Every loan, every engagement, every portfolio review is shaped by our commitment to sustainable, inclusive economic growth.

Social Impact

Every enterprise we back employs real people and anchors real supply chains. Ratelrock tracks job creation and retention at portfolio level, reported quarterly to investors. Our capital flows directly into productive economic activity.

Economic Inclusion

The enterprises we back represent an important layer of Kenya's economy, mid-market businesses that anchor supply chains, distribute goods to underserved communities, and provide market access to smallholder farmers and local manufacturers.

Governance Strengthening

Every enterprise undergoes a governance assessment at underwriting. Where gaps exist, our Strategic Capital Division implements board structures, shareholder agreements, financial reporting frameworks, and internal controls. Governance improvement is a defined investment objective.

Environmental Responsibility

Kenya's agribusiness sector, our primary focus, operates at the intersection of economic growth and environmental risk. We apply environmental risk screening at underwriting, assess climate exposure, and support businesses in adopting sustainable practices.

Primary Sector Focus

Agribusiness is
Kenya's backbone.

Kenya's agricultural sector contributes over 33% of GDP and employs more than 40% of the total population. It is the sector where structured credit, responsible governance, and active partnership create the most transformative impact.

Our capital flows into agribusiness processors, manufacturing operations, and the value chains that connect smallholder farmers to domestic and export markets, building resilient supply chains that Kenya's food security depends on.
