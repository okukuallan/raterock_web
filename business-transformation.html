Track 01

Distressed Business
Turnarounds.

Ratelrock embeds directly in the business — alongside management, with the board, in dialogue with creditors — to stabilise, restructure, and rebuild.

What a Turnaround Engagement Covers

Forensic financial and operational diagnostic — understanding the actual root cause, not just the presenting problem
Immediate cash flow stabilisation — protecting liquidity while the recovery plan is built
Management support and capability reinforcement — deploying our team alongside yours where gaps exist
Creditor engagement and debt restructuring — creating the breathing room the business needs
Balance sheet and capital structure optimisation — renegotiating, refinancing, and right-sizing for the recovery ahead
Governance and financial reporting strengthening — building the institutional infrastructure investors and lenders require
Capital solution — where bridge or restructuring capital is required, Ratelrock can provide it, separately evaluated and structured

Who This Is For

  • Businesses experiencing acute liquidity stress that need immediate stabilisation
  • Enterprises navigating covenant pressures with existing lenders
  • Businesses recovering from a significant operational event — loss of a major customer, management departure, fraud discovery, or supply chain failure
  • Shareholders and boards managing a portfolio company in distress who need outside expertise to lead the recovery

Discretion Guaranteed

All distressed engagements are handled with strict confidentiality. We do not share client information with third parties without explicit consent. For distressed situations, discretion is our default and our commitment.

Ratelrock Strategic Capital Division

Track 02

Growth-Stage
Transformation.

Governance gaps, incomplete records, and management structures that haven't caught up with business growth are solvable problems — not permanent barriers.

What We Deliver

Financial reporting professionalisation — management accounts, cash flow forecasting, budgeting, and internal controls built to institutional standards
Governance strengthening — board structuring, shareholder agreements, company secretarial, audit readiness, and compliance frameworks
Operational efficiency — supply chain optimisation, cost structure analysis, margin improvement, and process standardisation
Commercial finance preparation — financing memoranda, commercial bank introductions, loan package structuring, and negotiation support
Strategic growth advisory — market positioning, partnership development, M&A preparation, and expansion planning

Who This Is For

  • Owner-managed enterprises preparing for their first institutional credit or equity raise
  • Businesses undergoing ownership transition — MBO, MBI, or succession — that require professionalisation before financing
  • Enterprises that have outgrown their current management and governance infrastructure
  • Businesses that have received feedback from a financier on what needs to change before they can lend — and need expert hands to close those gaps

The Intersection

Transformation + Capital.
When both are needed.

Where a capital solution is required as part of the turnaround or growth plan, Ratelrock's Private Credit facility can be brought alongside the transformation engagement — separately evaluated, independently structured, and deployed when the business is ready to receive it responsibly.
