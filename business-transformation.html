Consultant & Advisory

We don't deliver
a report and leave.
We embed.

Across four practice areas, we provide the expertise, structure, and hands-on execution that Kenya's enterprises need whether facing distress, navigating a transaction, or building for the next stage of growth.

Our Practice Areas

Practice Area 01

Turnaround &
Restructuring.

A fundamentally sound business in difficulty is not a write-off. Ratelrock embeds directly in the business alongside management, with the board, and in dialogue with creditors to stabilise, restructure, and rebuild. We do not deliver a report and leave. We stay until the recovery is real.

What a turnaround engagement covers

Forensic financial and operational diagnostic identifying the actual root cause, not just the presenting problem
Immediate cash flow stabilisation and liquidity protection while the recovery plan is built
Creditor engagement and debt restructuring creating the breathing room the business needs
Balance sheet and capital structure optimisation renegotiating, refinancing, and right-sizing for the recovery ahead
Management support and capability reinforcement, deploying our team alongside yours where gaps exist
Governance and financial reporting strengthening, building the institutional infrastructure investors and lenders require

Who this is for

→Businesses experiencing acute liquidity stress that need immediate stabilisation
→Enterprises navigating creditor or covenant pressure with existing lenders
→Businesses recovering from loss of a major customer, fraud discovery, or supply chain failure
→Boards managing a portfolio company in distress who need outside expertise to lead the recovery

Capital bridge

Where bridge or restructuring capital is required, Ratelrock can provide it separately evaluated and structured through our Private Credit division.

Practice Area 02

Transaction
Advisory.

Transactions in the Kenyan mid-market require advisors who understand the complexity on the ground not just the theory of the deal. Ratelrock provides independent transaction advisory, bringing the financial rigour and operational insight that ensures our clients enter and exit transactions with full clarity on value, structure, and risk.

What we deliver

Buy-side: Target identification, financial due diligence, valuation, deal structuring, and negotiation support
Sell-side: Business preparation, information memorandum, buyer identification, negotiation, and full management of the sale process
MBO / MBI: Management buyouts and buy-ins structuring the transaction, organising finance, and managing the governance transition
Succession: Ownership succession planning and execution for founder-led businesses transitioning to next-generation management or external investors

Who this is for

→Owner-managed enterprises considering a sale, partial exit, or succession
→Businesses looking to acquire a competitor or enter a new market via acquisition
→Management teams seeking to buy out an existing owner or investor
→Founder-led businesses navigating ownership transfer to the next generation

Practice Area 03

Corporate Finance
Advisory.

Capital structure is strategy. How a business is financed determines what it can do, how fast it can move, and what it costs to stay in business. Ratelrock helps enterprises design, negotiate, and execute the capital structures that match their ambition.

What we deliver

Structuring and negotiating debt facilities including senior secured, mezzanine, and specialised instruments with existing and new lenders
Right-sizing the financial structure for the business's operating model, growth plan, and risk profile identifying and resolving structural inefficiencies
Building the financial package management accounts, cash flow models, financing memoranda, and covenant frameworks that institutional lenders require
Introductions to commercial banks, DFIs, and private credit providers, with structured support through the credit process

Who this is for

→Businesses with existing debt that is mis-priced, mis-structured, or constraining growth
→Enterprises preparing to approach a commercial bank or DFI for the first time
→Companies that have outgrown their current lender and need a broader financing relationship
→Businesses that need a credible, lender-ready financial package to unlock the next facility

Practice Area 04

Growth & Governance
Advisory.

Governance gaps, incomplete financial records, and management structures that haven't kept pace with business growth are solvable problems not permanent barriers. Ratelrock works directly with management to build the financial reporting, governance, and operational infrastructure that unlocks the next level of capital at better terms. This is not consulting at a distance. We build it with you.

What we deliver

Financial reporting professionalisation management accounts, cash flow forecasting, budgeting frameworks, and internal controls built to institutional standards
Governance strengthening board structuring, shareholder agreements, company secretarial, audit readiness, and compliance frameworks
Operational efficiency supply chain optimisation, cost structure analysis, margin improvement, and process standardisation
Strategic advisory market positioning, commercial strategy, partnership development, and M&A preparation

Who this is for

→Owner-managed enterprises preparing for their first institutional credit or equity raise
→Businesses undergoing MBO, MBI, or succession that require professionalisation before financing
→Enterprises that have outgrown their current management and governance infrastructure
→Businesses that have received lender feedback on what needs to change before they can access capital
