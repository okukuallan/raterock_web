Who We Fund

Real revenues. Real assets.
Real growth plans.

Our target market is enterprises with funding gaps between KES 50M and KES 500M operating across Kenya's productive sectors.

Segment 01

High-growth, asset-light enterprises

Businesses whose value lies in contracts, recurring revenue, intellectual property, and relationships rather than fixed assets. We understand how to underwrite intangible-heavy models.

Segment 02

Seasonal or project-based revenue cycles

Where standard amortisation schedules do not reflect actual cash generation patterns. We build repayment around your harvest, contract milestones, or seasonal cycles, not a generic template.

Segment 03

Complex capital structures

Strong cash flows that require subordinated capital structured around existing obligations. We work with complexity, not against it.

Segment 04

Acquisition & transition situations

Management buyouts, management buy-ins, ownership transitions, and expansion financing. We provide the specialist capital these events require.

Segment 05

Turnaround & special situations

Fundamentally sound businesses navigating a period of stress that require both capital and active support. This is where our credit judgment and our transformation capability come together. Learn about our Business Transformation service →

The Product Suite

Four structures.
One approach.

Each product is built around how businesses actually generate cash, not how lenders prefer to receive it.

Product 01

Senior Secured Lending

Secured against a wide asset base, property, equipment, contracts, receivables, inventory, and working capital, valued at realistic enforcement values. Repayment is built around your revenue cycle: interest-only periods, seasonal bullet repayments, and harvest-aligned structures are standard, not exceptions.

Download Brochure

Product 02

Asset Finance Lending

We finance critical income-generating equipment, including specialist or second-hand assets where the underwriting focus is on productive cash flow, not liquidation value alone. Sale-leaseback solutions available to unlock working capital from existing assets.

Download Brochure

Product 03

Mezzanine / Quasi-Equity

Growth capital structured as subordinated debt with an equity participation component. The entrepreneur retains majority ownership and control; Ratelrock participates in value creation through a performance-linked return structure. We typically take a board observer seat, providing strategic input without governance control. Patient, aligned capital for long-term value creation.

Download Brochure

Product 04

Bridge Loans & Special Situation Finance

Short-tenor capital for time-critical or structurally complex situations, pending a refinancing, awaiting a defined liquidity event, post-event recovery, or management transition. These engagements require our deepest credit judgment and most active partnership, and are structured and priced accordingly.

Beyond Disbursement

Active partnership
from day one.

Ratelrock's engagement does not end at disbursement. Every facility includes dedicated post-investment support through our Portfolio Consultant programme, active financial monitoring, regular strategic reviews, and hands-on governance support.

Our goal is not to be your permanent lender. It is to graduate you, to build the financial track record, governance infrastructure, and operational resilience that makes your business attractive to mainstream financing at a lower cost of capital.
