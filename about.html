Who We Are

Genuine transformation.

Ratelrock is East Africa's specialist private credit fund and business transformation practice. We provide structured debt capital and hands-on enterprise support, combining institutional credit rigour with a genuine transformation capability.

That combination is what makes us distinctly positioned in this market. When your enterprise needs a credit partner that truly understands its complexity, Ratelrock is where you start.

Ratelrock was built specifically for this market. We provide structured debt capital and active enterprise partnership to businesses at the stage where specialist expertise and flexible capital unlock the most value.

Fund Structure

Mauritius-domiciled.
Kenya-rooted.

Ratelrock is domiciled in Mauritius under an FSC-licensed fund structure, with a Kenya operational entity compliant with the CMA Kenya framework.

This dual structure provides institutional investors with a familiar, internationally recognised governance environment while our Kenya operations deliver local sourcing depth, sector expertise, and relationship infrastructure.
