Fund Structure

Mauritius-domiciled.
Kenya-rooted.

Ratelrock is domiciled in Mauritius under an FSC-licensed fund structure, with a Kenya operational entity compliant with the CMA Kenya framework.

This dual structure provides institutional investors with a familiar, internationally recognised governance environment while our Kenya operations deliver local sourcing depth, sector expertise, and relationship infrastructure.
